# **App Name**: Taat Elegance

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication: Enables users to register and log in securely via email/password or Google, integrated seamlessly with Firebase Authentication.
- Premium Product Catalog & Display: Showcases Taat Sharee products with immersive 3D rotatable imagery (using Three.js for interactive views), rich media (video, close-ups), comprehensive specifications, advanced filtering and sorting, and elegant product cards featuring hover effects.
- Interactive Shopping Cart Experience: Allows users to add, manage (adjust quantities, remove items), and review their selected items within a beautifully designed glass-panel shopping cart, complete with smooth animations, estimated delivery information, and coupon application.
- Streamlined Multi-Step Checkout: Guides users through a clear, multi-step checkout process for entering address details, selecting payment methods (bKash, Nagad, COD), reviewing their order summary, and a progress indicator for a smooth purchasing journey.
- Personalized User Dashboard: Provides a central, aesthetically pleasing hub for users to effortlessly manage their orders, track shipments, organize wishlists, update address books, submit product reviews, and save payment card information, all within glass-panel sections.
- Comprehensive Admin & Content Management: A secure and role-based administration panel (Super Admin, Product Manager, Order Manager) to add and manage detailed product listings (including multiple images, video URLs, and 30+ attributes), categories, customer reviews, orders, coupons, and artisan profiles, all powered by Firestore.
- Dynamic Wishlist Functionality: Enables users to save their favorite Taat Sharee for future reference, complete with engaging 'save to wishlist' animations and a 'heart burst' effect upon adding an item.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Soft Rose Pink (#E88CA8). This luxurious and feminine hue (HSL: 339, 61%, 73%) provides vibrancy, setting a premium and emotional tone that captures the essence of both heritage and modern boutique elegance.
- Background Color: Very Light Blush (#FAF0F4). A clean, soft, and modern canvas (HSL: 339, 15%, 98%) subtly complementing the primary rose pink by sharing its hue, yet heavily desaturated and bright to accentuate content while aligning with a light iOS-inspired aesthetic.
- Main Accent Color: Deep Wine / Maroon (#8B1E3F). This rich and deep accent (HSL: 341, 60%, 31%) provides a luxurious contrast to the soft rose primary. Its darker tone and increased saturation evoke premium elegance, perfect for key calls-to-action and important highlights.
- Highlight Accent: Royal Gold (#C6A75E). An additional accent color (HSL: 41, 56%, 63%) symbolizing luxury and sophistication, ideal for small decorative elements, special badging, or delicate lines, enhancing the premium feel.
- Text Primary Color: Dark Charcoal (#2A2A2A). A deep, achromatic tone (HSL: 0, 0%, 16%) that provides excellent readability and sharp contrast against light backgrounds, embodying sophistication and modernity without harshness.
- Headline Font: 'Belleza' (humanist sans-serif), offering a contemporary and art-inspired feel. Body Text Font: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif), chosen for its elegant, intellectual, and comfortable readability, complementing Belleza for extensive content like product descriptions.
- All icons will adhere to a soft-line minimal style, ensuring they are clean, modern, and harmonious with the elegant, glass-effect UI. Icons will reflect concepts related to heritage, quality, and boutique fashion.
